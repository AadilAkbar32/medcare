Sample journey

From your doorstep to a verified report.

01 / Book

Schedule online

Pick a test, a time window and your address. Confirmation arrives by SMS in minutes.

02 / Collect

Phlebotomist visits

A trained, badge-verified phlebotomist collects your sample at home or at the clinic.

03 / Transit

Cold-chain transport

Samples move to the lab in temperature-controlled carriers within the validated window.

04 / Analyze

Bench & QC

Automated analyzers run the test alongside daily internal quality controls.

05 / Report

Pathologist sign-off

A pathologist reviews and verifies every result before your digital report is released.
